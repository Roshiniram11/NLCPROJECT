Overview

Thermal Power Station II is the backbone of our company. With an annual turnover of more than Rs 1000 crores, TPS II caters to the requirements of the southern states of the country.

  • Total Capacity: 1470 MW (7 units of 210 MW each)
  • Initial Sanction: February 1978 (630 MW, 3 x 210 MW)
  • Capacity Enhancement: February 1983 (1470 MW, addition of 4 x 210 MW)
  • Synchronization Timeline: First Unit: March 1986, Last Unit: June 1993
  • Primary Fuel: Low calorific value/high moisture lignite from NLC-owned open-cast mines

Salient Features

1 First lignite-fired station with 210MW units
2 Station turnover of above Rs.1000 Crore for many years
3 Station with ISO 9001, 14001 & 45001 certifications
4 Station with highly skilled, dedicated, and motivated employees
5 Several steps towards energy conservation and cost control
6 Attaching utmost importance to the environment, with implementation of schemes on a continuous basis, towards eco-friendly safety measures
7 Proud of fine-tuning operations under ABT with maximum benefit to the company
8 Innovative and user-friendly applications in the area of computerization
